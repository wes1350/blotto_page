Blotto Fall 2018, Round 2: Win Harder

Submissions due 5pm EST Friday, Nov. 16

Remaining soldiers: 100

General Blotto Rules:

  • Tournament structure is round-robin. Your entry will play every other entry once.
  • Your score for a round is your average score against the rest of the entries.
  • Win a field by having more soldiers on that field than your opponent.
  • You may submit multiple entries, but only your last one will count (make sure the names match).

Submission Rules (red = requirement not satisfied):

  • 7 fields, exactly 100 soldiers
  • Field entries must be nonnegative integers
  • Entries must be nonincreasing from fields 1-7 (e.g. 40, 25, 25, 7, 2, 1, 0)

Round Scoring:

  • Each field is worth 1 point
  • Ties on a field award 1/2 point
  • Your score for a match is the square of the number of points you earn (e.g. 3 wins, 2 ties = 4 points = 16 score)

Scoring Example:

Ripka submits [20, 20, 20, 20, 10, 10, 0].
Willie submits [60, 25, 15, 0, 0, 0, 0].
Willie wins fields 1 and 2, as he sent more soldiers to each. This earns him 2 points.
Ripka wins fields 3 to 6, as he sent more soldiers to each. He earns 4 points for this.
Willie and Ripka tie on field 7, so they both earn 0.5 points for this.
In total, Ripka earns 4.5 points while Willie earns 2.5.
As a result, Ripka's score is (4.5)^2 = 20.25, and Willie's score is (2.5)^2 = 6.25.
