Round Rules (red = requirement not satisfied):

  • 5 fields, exactly 100 soldiers total
  • Field entries must be nonnegative integers
  • Entries must be nonincreasing from fields 1-5 (e.g. 40, 25, 25, 7, 3)

Round Scoring:

  • Each field is worth 1 point
  • Ties on a field award 1/2 point

Scoring Example:

Ripka submits [30, 30, 20, 20, 0].
Willie submits [20, 20, 20, 20, 20].
Ripka wins fields 1 and 2, as he sent more soldiers to each than Willie. This earns Ripka 2 points.
Ripka and Willie tie on fields 3 and 4, as they both sent 20 soldiers. They earn 1/2 point for each field.
Willie wins field 5, as he sent more soldiers than Ripka. He earns 1 point for this.
In total, Ripka earns 3 points while Willie earns 2.
