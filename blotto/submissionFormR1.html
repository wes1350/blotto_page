Blotto Fall 2018, Round 1: Scalability Costs

Submissions due 5pm EST Wednesday, Nov. 14

Remaining soldiers: 100

General Blotto Rules:

  • Tournament structure is round-robin. Your entry will play every other entry once.
  • Your score for a round is your average score against the rest of the entries.
  • Win a field by having more soldiers on that field than your opponent.
  • You may submit multiple entries, but only your last one will count (make sure the names match).

Submission Rules (red = requirement not satisfied):

  • 5 fields, UP TO 100 soldiers total (any number of soldiers from 0 to 100 is valid)
  • Field entries must be nonnegative integers

Round Scoring:

  • Fields 1 - 5 are worth 20, 25, 30, 35, 40 points, respectively
  • Ties on a field award 1/2 of that field's value
  • First 50 soldiers are free
  • Next 25 soldiers cost 1/2 point each
  • Next 25 soldiers cost 1 point each
  • NOTE: YOU DO NOT HAVE TO USE ALL 100 SOLDIERS

Scoring Example:

Ripka submits [30, 30, 20, 20, 0].
Willie submits [10, 10, 0, 20, 30].
Ripka wins fields 1, 2, and 3, as he sent more soldiers to each than Willie. This earns Ripka 20 + 25 + 30 = 75 points.
Ripka and Willie tie on field 4, as they both sent 20 soldiers. They earn 1/2 * 35 = 17.5 points each.
Willie wins field 5, as he sent more soldiers than Ripka. He earns 40 points for this.
However, since Ripka sent 100 soldiers, he pays 0.5 * 25 + 1 * 25 = 37.5 points as penalty.
Willie sent 70 soldiers, so he pays 0.5 * 20 = 10 points as penalty.
In total, Ripka scores 75 + 17.5 - 37.5 = 55 points, and Willie scores 17.5 + 40 - 10 = 47.5 points.
