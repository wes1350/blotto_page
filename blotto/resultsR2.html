Round Rules (red = requirement not satisfied):

  • 7 fields, exactly 100 soldiers
  • Field entries must be nonnegative integers
  • Entries must be nonincreasing from fields 1-7 (e.g. 40, 25, 25, 7, 2, 1, 0)

Round Scoring:

  • Each field is worth 1 point
  • Ties on a field award 1/2 point
  • Your score for a match is the square of the number of points you earn (e.g. 3 wins, 2 ties = 4 points = 16 score)

Scoring Example:

Ripka submits [30, 30, 25, 15, 0].
Willie submits [60, 25, 15, 0, 0].
Willie wins field 1, as he sent more soldiers. This earns him 1 point.
Ripka wins fields 2 to 4, as he sent more soldiers to each. He earns 3 points for this.
Willie and Ripka tie on field 5, so they both earn 0.5 points for this.
In total, Ripka earns 3.5 points while Willie earns 1.5.
As a result, Ripka's score is (3.5)^2 = 12.25, and Willie's score is (1.5)^2 = 2.25.
