Blotto Fall 2018, Round 0

Rules (red = requirement not satisfied):

  • Exactly 100 soldiers
  • Field entries must be nonnegative integers
  • Entries must be nonincreasing from fields 1-5

Scoring:

  • Highest average score in the round-robin tournament wins the round.
  • 1 point per field win, 0.5 points per field tie.
