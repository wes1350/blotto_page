Blotto Fall 2018, Round 5: More Scalability Costs

Submissions due 5pm EST Friday, Nov. 23

Remaining soldiers: 100

General Blotto Rules:

  • Tournament structure is round-robin. Your entry will play every other entry once.
  • Your score for a round is your average score against the rest of the entries.
  • Win a field by having more soldiers on that field than your opponent.
  • You may submit multiple entries, but only your last one will count (make sure the names match).

Submission Rules (red = requirement not satisfied):

  • 7 fields, exactly 100 soldiers
  • Field entries must be nonnegative integers
  • Entries must be nonincreasing from fields 1-4 and 5-6 (e.g. {25, 10, 10, 5}, {15, 15}, 20)

Round Scoring:

  • Fields 1-7 worth {4, 4, 4, 4, 7, 7, 10} points, respectively
  • Ties on a field award 1/2 of the field value
  • You pay SQRT(# soldiers sent) penalty on each field

Scoring Example:

Ripka submits [16, 16, 16, 16, 9, 9, 18].
Willie submits [25, 16, 9, 4, 36, 1, 9].
Willie wins field 1, winning 4 points.
They tie on field 2, winning 2 points each.
Ripka wins fields 3 and 4, winning 8 points.
Willie wins field 5, winning 7 points.
Ripka wins field 6, winning 7 points.
Ripka also wins field 7, winning 10 points.

However, Ripka pays penalty for each field, totaling [sqrt(16)*4 + sqrt(9)*2 + sqrt(18) ~ 24.24.]
Similarly, Willie ends up paying in 24 points penalty.
In total, Ripka scores 2 + 8 + 7 + 10 - 24.24 ~ 2.76 points, while Willie scores 5 + 2 + 7 - 24 = -10 points.
