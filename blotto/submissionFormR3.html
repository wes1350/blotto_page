Blotto Fall 2018, Round 3: Transportation Costs

Submissions due 5pm EST Sunday, Nov. 18

Remaining soldiers: 100

General Blotto Rules:

  • Tournament structure is round-robin. Your entry will play every other entry once.
  • Your score for a round is your average score against the rest of the entries.
  • Win a field by having more soldiers on that field than your opponent.
  • You may submit multiple entries, but only your last one will count (make sure the names match).

Submission Rules (red = requirement not satisfied):

  • 10 fields, exactly 100 soldiers
  • Field entries must be nonnegative integers
  • Entries must be nonincreasing from fields 1-10 (e.g. 40, 25, 15, 7, 4, 4, 3, 2, 0, 0)

Round Scoring:

  • Each field is worth 3 points
  • Tying on a field awards half the points (3/2) to each player
  • Sending 0 soldiers to a field awards 0 points, even in ties, but costs 0 points
  • Sending 1 or more soldiers to a field costs 1 point, but makes you eligible for earning points on that field

Scoring Example:

Ripka submits [20, 10, 10, 10, 10, 10, 10, 10, 10, 0].
Willie submits [60, 30, 10, 0, 0, 0, 0, 0, 0, 0, 0].
Willie wins fields 1 and 2, as he sent more soldiers to each. This earns him 6 points.
Ripka and Willie tie on field 3, so they both earn 1.5 points for this.
Ripka wins fields 4-9 amd earns 18 points as a result.
Ripka and Willie, as they both sent 0 soldiers to field 10, both earn 0 points for field 10.
Since Ripka sent soldiers to 9 fields, he pays 9 points in penalty. Similarly, Willie pays 3 points in penalty.
In total, Ripka's score is 1.5 + 18 - 9 = 10.5, and Willie's score is 6 + 1.5 - 3 = 4.5.
